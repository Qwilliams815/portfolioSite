From Peanuts to Programming

Howdy, I’m Brian! A nut butter media producer turned AV technician specialist turned junior web developer. It’s been a long, rather unconventional road, but I’m thrilled to be just getting started.

I began my programming journey early 2020 during Covid-19 lockdowns. Upon becoming furloughed from my job, I decided to fill my time learning Python and quickly became addicted to learning anything and everything programming. Using the internet's vast assortment of free classes, certificate programs, and tutorials, I began plotting out potential career paths, ultimately deciding on Web Development. Fast forward a year and I’ve been honing my skills ever since, building out challenging passion projects and growing as a developer.

Moving forward, I am excited to join a team and contribute to creating real world solutions. If you can help make that happen, I look forward to hearing from you. Thanks for reading!
